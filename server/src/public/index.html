Up and running
in minutes.

Three steps to give your agents a verified identity and audit trail.

01

Register your agent

Install the SDK and register your agent with a name and declared scope. Receive a cryptographic DID and secret.

npm install aria-sdk
02

Track every action

Wrap your agent's actions. ARIA verifies scope, signs events, and detects anomalies in real time — server-side.

agent.track('action', fn)
03

Prove compliance

Show regulators and auditors a verified history of every action your agent took — with cryptographic proof.
