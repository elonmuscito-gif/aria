Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

ARIA ("we", "us", or "our") is operated by Josías Hernández, based in Cali, Colombia. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account or use ARIA, we collect:

  • Account information: your name and email address
  • Authentication data: hashed passwords (we never store plaintext passwords)
  • Agent data: names, declared scopes, and cryptographic identifiers (DIDs) of agents you register
  • Event data: actions, outcomes, timestamps, and signatures submitted via the SDK or API
  • Technical data: IP addresses (for rate limiting and security), request logs

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the ARIA service
  • To authenticate your account and secure your API keys
  • To compute trust scores and detect anomalies for your agents
  • To send security alerts and webhook notifications you configure
  • To send transactional emails (account confirmation, 2FA codes)
  • To prevent abuse, fraud, and unauthorized access

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway (United States). We implement the following security measures:

  • Passwords are hashed with bcrypt (cost factor 10) — never stored in plaintext
  • API keys are hashed with bcrypt and indexed via SHA-256
  • Secrets are encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM
  • All traffic is encrypted in transit via HTTPS/TLS
  • Event data is stored append-only and cannot be deleted or modified

4. Data Retention

  • Account data: retained while your account is active
  • Event data: retained indefinitely (audit trail is immutable by design)
  • Anomaly data: archived after 90 days, never deleted
  • IP logs: cleared every 10 minutes from memory (not persisted to DB)

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with third parties except:

  • Railway: infrastructure provider (database and hosting)
  • Resend: email delivery provider (receives your email address to send transactional emails)
  • Legal requirements: if required by law or to protect our rights

6. Webhooks

If you configure webhook URLs, ARIA will send event data to those URLs. You are responsible for the security and privacy practices of your webhook endpoints.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data (note: event audit trails cannot be deleted by design)
  • Export your agent and event data

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@ariatrust.org.

8. Cookies and Local Storage

The ARIA dashboard uses browser localStorage to store your API key and basic session information. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

9. Children

ARIA is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of significant changes via email.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests:

  • Email: privacy@ariatrust.org
  • Website: ariatrust.org
